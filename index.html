FAQ

Lash Extensions

How long do lash extensions last?

Lash extensions last through the natural lash cycle — typically 2–4 weeks before a fill is needed. With proper care, you can maintain them indefinitely with regular fills.

What's the difference between Classic, Hybrid, and Volume?

Classic: One extension per natural lash — a natural, everyday look. Hybrid: A mix of classic and volume for added drama without going full glam. Volume: 3–6 extensions per natural lash for a full, fluffy look.

How should I prepare for my appointment?

Come with clean, makeup-free eyes. Avoid using oil-based products around the eye area before your appointment.

Lash Lift

How should I prepare for a lash lift?

Come without eye makeup. After your treatment, keep lashes dry for 24 hours for best results.

How long does a lash lift last?

A lash lift lasts 6–8 weeks. Use Elleeplex or Elleevate serum daily ($40) to maintain and nourish your lashes. Tint lasts up to 4 weeks.

Brow Lamination

How should I prepare for brow lamination?

Come with clean brows, already shaped if possible. Avoid any brow products the day of your appointment.

How long does brow lamination last?

Brow lamination lasts up to 6 weeks. The treatment makes your brow hairs malleable — use gel or brow soap to hold your preferred style.
