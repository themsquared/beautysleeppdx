The Complete Guide to Lash Extensions & Lash Lifts in Portland, Oregon

Everything you need to know before booking the best lash extensions or lash lift in Portland — and why Beauty Sleep Studio is the studio to choose.

What Are Lash Extensions?

Lash extensions are individual synthetic (or silk) lashes applied one-by-one to your natural lashes by a licensed professional. They add length, fullness, and curl so you can skip mascara and strip lashes. In Portland, the most popular options are classic lash extensions (one extension per lash, natural look), hybrid lash extensions (mix of classic and volume), and volume lash extensions (multiple lightweight fans per lash for a full, fluffy look). At Beauty Sleep Studio in Nob Hill, we offer all three — full sets from $180–$260 and fills from $80.

Lash Extensions vs. Lash Lift: Which Is Right for You?

Lash extensions add new lashes to yours; they're customizable and can be natural or dramatic. You'll need fills every 2–3 weeks. Lash lift (sometimes called a "lash perm") curls your existing natural lashes upward for 6–8 weeks — no extensions, lower maintenance. Many Portland clients who want a low-maintenance look choose a lash lift and tint ($100 at Beauty Sleep Studio); those who want maximum impact choose volume or hybrid lash extensions. You can't do both at once — but we'll help you pick the best option for your lifestyle.

Why Choose a Licensed Esthetician for Lashes in Portland?

Oregon requires lash artists to be licensed (esthetician or cosmetologist). Going to a licensed pro means proper sanitation, safe adhesives, and training in eye anatomy. Haley at Beauty Sleep Studio has been a licensed esthetician since 2008 and specializes exclusively in lashes and brows — so you're not just getting "someone who does lashes," you're getting one of Portland's most experienced lash artists. That's why we're consistently named one of the best lash extension studios in Portland and Nob Hill.

FAQ

Lash Extensions

How long do lash extensions last?

Lash extensions last through the natural lash cycle — typically 2–4 weeks before a fill is needed. With proper care, you can maintain them indefinitely with regular fills.

What's the difference between Classic, Hybrid, and Volume?

Classic: One extension per natural lash — a natural, everyday look. Hybrid: A mix of classic and volume for added drama without going full glam. Volume: 3–6 extensions per natural lash for a full, fluffy look.

How should I prepare for my appointment?

Come with clean, makeup-free eyes. Avoid using oil-based products around the eye area before your appointment.

Where is the best place to get lash extensions in Portland?

Beauty Sleep Studio PDX in Nob Hill (1308 NW 20th Ave, Suite 4) is Portland's premier lash extension studio. Licensed esthetician Haley has specialized in lash extensions, lash lifts, and brow lamination since 2008. Book online for Tuesday–Friday, 10am–8pm.

Are lash extensions safe?

Yes. When applied by a licensed professional like Haley at Beauty Sleep Studio, lash extensions are safe. Each extension is bonded to a single natural lash (not the skin) with professional-grade adhesive. Haley has been a licensed esthetician since 2008.

Do I need a lash fill or full set?

If you're new to extensions or have let them grow out completely, you need a full set ($180–$260). If you still have 40–50% of your extensions, a fill ($80–$120) will restore fullness. Most Portland clients book fills every 2–3 weeks.

What are the best lash extensions for a natural look?

Classic lash extensions (one extension per natural lash) give the most natural look. At Beauty Sleep Studio PDX, classic full sets are $180; Haley customizes length and curl to your eye shape so they look like your lashes, only better.

Lash Lift

How should I prepare for a lash lift?

Come without eye makeup. After your treatment, keep lashes dry for 24 hours for best results.

How long does a lash lift last?

A lash lift lasts 6–8 weeks. Use Elleeplex or Elleevate serum daily ($40) to maintain and nourish your lashes. Tint lasts up to 4 weeks.

What's the difference between a lash lift and lash extensions?

A lash lift curls your existing natural lashes (like a perm) — no lashes are added; it lasts 6–8 weeks. Lash extensions add individual synthetic lashes to each natural lash for length and volume; they require fills every 2–3 weeks. We offer both at Beauty Sleep Studio in Portland.

How much do lash lifts cost in Portland?

At Beauty Sleep Studio, lash lift with tint is $100, lash lift only is $85, and lash tint only is $30. Results last 6–8 weeks. Book online at beautysleepstudiopdx.com.

Brow Lamination

How should I prepare for brow lamination?

Come with clean brows, already shaped if possible. Avoid any brow products the day of your appointment.

How long does brow lamination last?

Brow lamination lasts up to 6 weeks. The treatment makes your brow hairs malleable — use gel or brow soap to hold your preferred style.

Is brow lamination worth it?

Brow lamination gives you fuller, styled brows for up to 6 weeks without daily product. It's ideal if you want a lifted, fluffy brow look. We offer brow lamination at Beauty Sleep Studio — mention it in your booking notes or ask Haley during your lash appointment.

How do I find a good lash artist in Portland?

Look for a licensed esthetician with years of experience, a dedicated studio (not a shared chair), and a portfolio you love. Beauty Sleep Studio PDX in Nob Hill is run by Haley, licensed since 2008, with 18+ years specializing in lashes. Book a full set or consultation online.
